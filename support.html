The Magic Diary for iPad

Write on the page with Apple Pencil. When your pen rests, your ink dissolves into the paper, the diary thinks, and an answer writes itself back in ink — then fades away.

Need help? Email info@tikseer.com and we will get back to you.

Requirements

  • An Apple Intelligence–capable iPad (iPad Air 5th generation or newer)
  • iPadOS 26 or later
  • Apple Intelligence turned on in Settings
  • Apple Pencil (or another stylus)

Common questions

The diary says Apple Intelligence is unavailable.

Open the iPad's Settings app, go to Apple Intelligence & Siri, and make sure Apple Intelligence is turned on. It may need to finish downloading the first time. The diary also has its own Use Apple Intelligence switch in Magic Diary Settings — check that it is on.

The diary cannot read my handwriting.

Try writing a little larger and leaving a small gap between words. English is the most reliably recognized language. If a page is not recognized, your ink stays on the page so you can try again without losing it.

How do I make the diary wait longer before it answers?

In Magic Diary Settings, adjust Wait after writing. You can also change the reply handwriting, the fade speed, line spacing, and whether writing lines are shown.

Can I change the diary's personality?

Yes. In Settings you can choose Warm Keeper, The Oracle, The Riddle, or Playful Sprite — each has its own voice and colour. Choose Custom to write a personality of your own.

Where are my conversations stored?

Only on your iPad. Nothing is uploaded. Open Pages to read, search, or delete saved pages, or turn off Remember conversations in Settings so nothing is saved at all.

Does the diary use the internet?

Not unless you turn on Search the internet. When that setting is on, the diary may send a short search query to DuckDuckGo or Wikipedia to look something up. Everything else stays on your iPad, and the app works fully offline.
